Secure, automated parking management for businesses. Optimize your parking space with real-time monitoring and data-driven insights.

The Parking Challenge

Parking lots face significant challenges in monitoring occupancy, tracking vehicles, and collecting accurate data, leading to inefficient space utilization and revenue loss.

Unsupervised Lots

Without proper monitoring, parking lots become difficult to manage, leading to unauthorized usage and security concerns.

Inefficient Parking

Drivers waste time searching for available spots, causing frustration and increased traffic congestion.

Data Collection

Traditional parking systems lack accurate data collection methods, making it difficult to optimize space usage and revenue.

Our Solution

VehiScan-Tap combines RFID technology, IoT sensors, and AI analytics to create a comprehensive smart parking management system.

RFID Security System

Uses RFID tags in vehicles and scanners at parking areas for secure access control.

IoT Vehicle Tracking

Enables real-time tracking of location, speed, and vehicle condition.

AI-Powered Analytics

Provides data-driven insights for optimizing parking layout and resource allocation.

Key Features

Our comprehensive solution offers everything you need for efficient parking management.

RFID Access Control

Secure entry and exit with RFID tags for authorized vehicles only.

Smart Slot Management

Real-time monitoring of available parking slots with guided navigation.

Automated Payment

Contactless payment processing based on actual parking duration.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive reports and insights on parking usage patterns.

Vehicle Tracking

Monitor vehicle movement within the parking area for enhanced security.

Mobile Dashboard

Access all features and data from your smartphone or tablet.

How It Works

Our system is designed to be simple yet powerful, providing a seamless experience for both operators and users.

1

Vehicle Registration

Vehicles are registered in the system and equipped with RFID tags for identification.

2

Automated Entry/Exit

RFID scanners at entry/exit points authenticate vehicles and manage access control.

3

Real-time Monitoring

IoT sensors track vehicle movement and parking slot occupancy in real-time.
